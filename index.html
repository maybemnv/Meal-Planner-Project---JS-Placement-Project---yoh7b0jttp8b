About This Meal Planner

This meal planner uses the Harris-Benedict equation to calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and daily calorie needs based on your height, weight, age, gender, and activity level.

It then generates a personalized daily meal plan with breakfast, lunch, and dinner options that match your calorie requirements.

The meal data is provided by the Spoonacular API.

How to use:

  1. Enter your height, weight, age, gender, and activity level
  2. Click "Generate Meals" to get your personalized meal plan
  3. Click "Get Recipe" on any meal to see the detailed recipe
  4. Save meals you like to your favorites
